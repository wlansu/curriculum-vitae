Personal Strengths

I am dependable, I work hard to finish a project on time, failing that I inform the stakeholders in a timely fashion so we can resolve any problems that are interfering with the completion of the project.

I'm easy to work with and flexible when possible. I tend to ask the hard questions such as: "Why are we doing this, and is it really necessary?" Though not always appreciated it is meant to prevent feature creep and bloat, which are very real dangers in software development.
